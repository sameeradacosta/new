About Us

Costa Fabrica is a premier event planning and rental service based in Goa. We specialize in creating unforgettable experiences, combining elegance, creativity, and professionalism to make every occasion truly magical. Whether it’s weddings, birthdays, corporate events, or intimate gatherings, we provide high-quality furniture, decor, catering, and event services. Our dedicated team works closely with clients to ensure seamless planning, execution, and a stress-free celebration. At Costa Fabrica, we turn your vision into reality and craft memories that last a lifetime.
